About Me

Hi there!

"Everyone has a story, and here's mine. I'm Tanzeem. Born in a scenic place called Ooty, My journey into design wasn't straightforward. Initially pushed into engineering by various reasons, I was determined to follow my passion for design.

Despite limited resources, I deived into Game Design and found myself in PowerPoint design. My break came at Mckinsey & Company, where I honed my design skills. I ventured into entrepreneurship, but the pandemic brought unexpected challenges.

Now, as a UI/UX manager at Solulever, I lead a creative team. Beyond design, I mentor aspiring designers through my YouTube channel. My journey is all about determination and discovering my true calling in the design world."
